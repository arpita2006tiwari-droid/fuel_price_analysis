## 📄 Resume Bullet Points (ATS Optimized)
- **Developed** an end-to-end data processing pipeline in Python to transform 50+ years of crude oil data into a regional fuel price dataset with 10k+ records.
- **Architected** an interactive Streamlit dashboard featuring multi-dimensional filtering, KPI tracking, and real-time Plotly visualizations for Petrol and Diesel trends.
- **Engineered** a robust PostgreSQL database schema and optimized query performance using composite indexing and window functions for historical trend analysis.
- **Performed** exploratory data analysis (EDA) using Pandas and Seaborn to identify regional price disparities and price volatility spikes with 95%+ correlation to crude oil shifts.
- **Derived** 10+ actionable business insights regarding regional taxation impacts and fuel price convergence, providing a data-driven overview of energy cost inflation.
